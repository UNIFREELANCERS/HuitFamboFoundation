ABOUT US

We are a movement for change.

Huit Fambo Foundation is a youth-led community organization based in Kibera, using the power of dance, mentorship, and environmental action to transform lives and build a more sustainable future.

We are more than a dance crew—we are a movement for change.

WHO WE ARE

Passion Meets Purpose

We are a passionate group of young people committed to empowering the youth, creating safe spaces, and tackling the social and environmental challenges affecting our community.

Through creativity, advocacy, and hands-on community engagement, we strive to inspire long-lasting impact.

OUR MISSION

Empower. Reform. Sustain.

Our mission is to empower and reform the youth by providing a safe, nurturing, and creative environment.

Through the transformative power of dance, we aim to raise awareness, educate, advocate, and give a voice to those unheard.

We are equally committed to environmental conservation through waste collection, sorting, and recycling initiatives.

Together, we can build a brighter future for youth and a sustainable world for all.
