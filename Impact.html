IMPACT IN ACTION

Stories from the ground

Safe Mentorship Circles

Weekly sessions across three schools equip teens with life skills, menstrual health knowledge, and leadership confidence.

Waste Wise Saturdays

Teams sweep through 40 households, collecting, sorting, and selling recyclables to fund school supplies and sanitary kits.

Dance for Change Tours

Our dance crew performs narrative pieces spotlighting gender equality, inspiring thousands during community forums.

Library & Safe Space

In partnership with Masaku Lions Club, 35–40 children access books, tutoring, and counseling every week.

Spotlight

"Before Huit Fambo, I skipped school to avoid bullying. The mentorship circle helped me speak up and now I'm a prefect leading clean-up drives." – Michelle, Form 2

Michelle’s story mirrors hundreds of young people who now connect their creativity with civic responsibility. Our ecosystem ensures that once a youth joins Huit Fambo, they stay supported through mentorship, expression, and economic opportunities.
