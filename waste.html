How it works

Waste Wise Saturdays

Our squads patrol assigned routes, collecting household waste, teaching sorting practices, and salvaging recyclables for sale. Proceeds support sanitary kits and library supplies.

Weekly Routine

  • 07:30 — Team assembly and route briefing
  • 09:00 — Door-to-door collection and household chats
  • 11:30 — Sorting, weighing, and recording
  • 13:00 — Debrief and community share-back
